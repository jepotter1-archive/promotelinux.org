Linux

A free alternative to Windows and macOS

Free of Malware

Malware Amounts by Operating System

Windows and macOS are full of malware — malicious programs that sneak on to your computer and display annoying ads, spy on you, demand a ransom or even break your computer. Linux is free of most malware, so you can browse the internet freely without worrying about accidentally downloading malware.

Source: AV-Test 2015/16 Security Report

Free & Open Source

Linux is Free/Libre and Open Source Software. Unlike Windows and macOS, whose sole purpose is to make a profit off you, Linux wants to create the best possible experience for its users. Linux allows you to examine what it does and how it is done, so you can sleep easy knowing that your computer is not spying on you in any way. If you disagree with a feature, you can even modify it to suit your needs.

Plus, Linux is free, now and always. And you'll get all future updates, no matter how old your computer is.
